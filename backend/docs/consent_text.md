## LiveMap Consent Text (Short UI Copy)

Use this short copy in the LiveMap UI whenever you ask a user to enable broadcasting of their intent and approximate location:

> **LiveMap — share location & intent (15 minutes)**  
> Share my approximate location and service request for up to 15 minutes so nearby providers can offer to serve me. Providers will not see my name or exact location until I confirm a booking. I can revoke sharing at any time.

For longer‑form explanations, link to `docs/privacy_policy.md` from your UI (for example, “View full privacy policy”).
